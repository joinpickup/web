Mission
Who are we and what are we about?
  • Who are we?
    We are a small team of three based out of New Haven CT. We are focused on helping foster local communities.
  • What are we trying to do?
    We want to build tools to provide the structure, community and purpose that is typically found in college, church or camp. Not everyone has access to those opportunities, so we want to provide the resources and events to help local communities thrive.
  • Why are we doing this?
    • - Everyone deserves to feel like they belong.
    • - Community is becoming harder to find.
    • - Too many companies take advantage of people for profit
    We want to prove that a company can put people first. Our main metric for success is lasting connections. If we can help even two people connect who wouldn't have connected without us, we acomplished our mission.
  • How Do We Make Money
    Currently? We don't. We have plans to release some software products that we will offer a fremium service for, but until then we are funded through donations.
  • Core Values
    • - People Over Profit
    • - Lifelong Learning
    • - Empowering Individuals

Mission
Who are we and what are we about?
  • What are we trying to do?
    We want to build tools to provide structure, community and the purpose that is found in opportunities like College and Church. Not everyone has access to those resources, so we want to provide the opportunities for those local communities to thrive.
  • Why are we doing this?
    We believe that everyone deserves to feel like they belong. Belonging comes from community. Intentional, lasting community is becoming harder and harder to find. Our goal is to make that process easier for everyone. If we can help even two people connect who wouldn't have connected without us, we acomplished our mission.
  • How Do We Make Money
    Currently? We don't. We have plans to release some software products that we will offer a fremium service for, but until then we are funded through donations.

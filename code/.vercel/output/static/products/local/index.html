Local
A digital bullitin board
  • The Problem
    In a digital age, people feel less connected to their communities.
  • The Solution
    Design an app to help people connect with others nearby over shared passions. No commitment, no pressure, no events, just people connecting with people.
  • How It Works
    Filter your search for a specific interest. Once you find a post that you like, message that person. That message disappears until they decide to respond. Once they respond you can message about getting together.
    You can also save a search so that if someone nearby creats a post that matches your filters you will get notified even when you aren't in the app.
    If you decide you want to post, tag it with an interest and others can find you. Eventually you'll get messages, accept or deny those messages and get together.
  • Features
    • - Posts
    • - Saved Searches
    • - Custom Filtering
    • - Intentional Messaging
    • - Anonymous Until You Connect
